FRequently asked questions
How can I join the platform?

Download the app from the link on the website and create your own account to start trading on your opinions.

How can I earn money?

Once you have registered, you can browse through a variety of events. Based on your expertise, understanding and analysis, you can put in your money in the event of your choice. Wait for the event to settle to either earn or learn.

What to do if my opinion didn't match?

You can place your order and wait for the counter-party to challenge your side Or you can reassess the scenario and enter a more suitable price and quantity.

Can I withdraw money from the wallet?

First, complete your KYC and fill in your bank account details to instantly withdraw your money. You can only withdraw earned money.

What’s the difference between investable money and earned money? 

Investable money is the total money that can be used to place a trade but can’t be withdrawn fully. However, earned money is the net money won in trades and can be withdrawn.

How is the price decided?

The price shown is the one at which the last trade is executed. Now as the supply and demand changes, it gets reflected into the price. This makes it similar to other economics markets. i.e Prices are not governed by the platform but by the people who are placing their trades.

When will I receive my winning money?

Once the event is settled, money will be credited into your wallet (if you win) and get reflected into earned money. Then, you can withdraw your money instantly if your KYC is completed.

What are private events? How can I create private events?

Private events are events that you can create on your own by customising existing events. You can share the link of private events with your friends/colleagues and trade privately on it.
